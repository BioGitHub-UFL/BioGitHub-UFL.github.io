29 January 2015: Run and optimize your R scripts on the cluster/ Visualizing results with ggplot2

Many of us leverage R packages or write our own R scripts, but sometimes computational costs are demanding or we want to explore uncertainty in our data. To kick off biogithub, we'll discuss running R scripts on a cluster followed by turning our results into slick figures for publication with ggplot2.
